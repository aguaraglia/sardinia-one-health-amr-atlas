PUBLIC MISSION

Make AMR evidence understandable without simplifying beyond what the data support.

The atlas brings together curated literature, surveillance, and publishable environmental or animal-health evidence, always stating source, geographic scale and interpretation limits.

PublicOne HealthReproducible

Purpose

A territorial resource for research, education and dialogue across human, animal and environmental health. It is not a clinical tool for individual patients.

Transparency

Every record states its source, period, geographic scale and the cautions needed to interpret the finding.

Privacy by design

Sample coordinates, farms, businesses, discharge details and other sensitive data are excluded from GitHub Pages.

Updates

New sources are included only after triage, bibliographic verification and a public-safety check.

Data governance

The public version uses regional, municipal or facility-level aggregation only where supported by the source. Missing points do not mean absence of AMR.

Attribution and reuse

The atlas has its own licence and citation file; third-party sources retain their respective terms of use.
